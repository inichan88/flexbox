Astrology is the study of the movements of celestial bodies (specifically the ones in our solar system) and how they relate to our lives and events on Earth. Join our newsletter to have weekly peeks into the future!

Some random information.

Electional Astrology - pick the best date for your big plans. Whether that's your marriage, vacations, buying your house or a job interview, you name it!

Horary Astrology - do you have a big question preoccupying your mind? Send that question to the universe and let it answer you at that exact moment!

Natal Astrology - find out how the stars aligned the moment you were born, and how to best navigate your life according to their influence.

Sinastry - are you two a match? Sinastry helps you understand how better you can relate with your partner and what are your strengths and weaknesses together.
